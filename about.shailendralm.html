Shailendra Photography

We are a photography division of DAM GLOBAL HOLDINGS (PTY) LTD, that specializes in portrait, events, and lifestyle photography for individuals couples and businesses.

Shailendra Photography was founded by Decent Andrew Moyana (formally known as Lee Milcus) an experienced and talented photographer with a passion for capturing special moments. Lee has a proven track record in the industry and has built a team of skilled photographers who share his vision and commitment to excellence. The team also includes professionals in marketing and customer service to ensure a seamless experience for our clients.

Our vision
We aim to become the go-to photography company in Cape Town, known for our exceptional quality, personalized service, and commitment to customer satisfaction. We envision expanding our services to other cities in South Africa and establishing ourselves as a trusted brand in the photography industry.
